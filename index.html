About Tether USD

Tether USD (USDT) is a BEP20 token built on Binance Smart Chain, designed to maintain a 1:1 peg with the U.S. Dollar. It provides stability, transparency, and interoperability for decentralized finance ecosystems.

As a stable digital asset, Tether USD is optimized for DeFi applications, offering efficient transfers, liquidity pools, and compatibility with leading DEX platforms like PancakeSwap.
